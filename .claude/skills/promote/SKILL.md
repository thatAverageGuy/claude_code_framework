---

Promotion strategy tied to real milestones, not vanity metrics.

## Process

1. **Understand project and audience**
   - Read discovery findings and strategy docs
   - What's actually built and working?
   - Who would care about this and where do they hang out?

2. **Research platforms**
   Use researcher agent to investigate:
   - Where does the target audience spend time?
   - What content performs well on each platform?
   - What are successful similar projects doing?

3. **Create content plan**
   Write to `docs/strategy/content_plan.md`:

   ```markdown
   # Content & Promotion Plan

   ## Target Audience
   - Primary: [who] — [where they are]
   - Secondary: [who] — [where]

   ## Content Pillars
   - [Pillar 1]: [what topics, why they resonate]
   - [Pillar 2]: [what topics, why]

   ## Platform Strategy
   ### LinkedIn
   - Content type: [what works here]
   - Cadence: [realistic frequency]
   - Example post topics: [3-5 specific ideas tied to milestones]

   ### Twitter/X
   - Content type: [threads, updates, engagement]
   - Cadence: [realistic]

   ### Reddit
   - Relevant subreddits: [specific, with rules noted]
   - Approach: [value-first, not promotional]

   ### Blog/Substack
   - Article topics: [specific, tied to expertise demonstrated]
   - Cadence: [realistic — quality over quantity]

   ## Content Calendar
   | Week | Milestone | Content | Platform | Type |
   |------|-----------|---------|----------|------|

   ## Quick-Start Drafts
   [2-3 ready-to-use content pieces for immediate posting]
   ```

4. **Create actionable content**
   - Draft actual posts, not just plans
   - Tie content to real project milestones
   - Write blog outlines with key points

## Rules
- Authenticity over polish — no fake enthusiasm
- Cadence must be realistic (don't plan daily posts if unsustainable)
- Content tied to real milestones, not manufactured events
- No engagement bait or shallow marketing
- Position honestly based on genuine substance
